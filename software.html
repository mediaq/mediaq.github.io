SOFTWARE

By a twist of fate, I started my career from a drawing board. A couple years of traditional manual drafting helped me to cultivate several extremely useful personal qualities for efficient work process organization, sense for details, methodologically consistent, and strategic approach to decision-making. Although, today I am a professional user of 3D detailing software, I still strongly believe that using "old school" methods made me a highly effective detailer.

Over the last decade, my detailing abilities have evolved to one of the most powerful software, Tekla Structures. This detailing software helps avoid design errors and conflicts in the finished project and significantly enhances performance, especially dealing with geometrically complex structures created by architects.

Striving to stay ahead of the constantly evolving industry, I continually invest in upgrading my technology, update my knowledge of the latest trends, carefully explore powerful features and advantages of Tekla Structures. I do not cut corners when it comes to investing in my professional development.

For some client needs, I also use AutoCAD, which is still handy for small projects or for improving drawing output from Tekla Structures. Combining both of these instruments I can achieve excellent quality for each drawing.
