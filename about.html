ABOUT ME

In 2008, at the age of 21, soon after graduating from College, I started my career as a drafting technician in the construction department of PJSC Dneprovsky Integrated Iron & Steel Works.

Working in this position and studying on a part-time basis, I graduated in 2011, from the Kyiv National University of Construction and Architecture and was awarded a сivil engineering degree with a focus on steel structures.

During the next two years, I worked as a structural engineer designing “peripheral” steel, RC, and geotechnical structures of blast furnaces, steelmaking, and hot rolling mill complexes within the modernization process of metallurgical plant infrastructure. I was also widely involved in renovation projects of structural strengthening and rehabilitation of existing, distressed structures, especially crane runway beams.

In 2013, I began working as a steel detailer for one of the most technologically advanced Ukrainian steel fabrication company’s, SteelTech, in Brovary. It allowed me to gain a deeper insight into the steel fabrication industry, manufacturing processes, up-to-date machinery, and equipment.

At the end of 2013, I became self-employed to provide professional, independent steel detailing services and started to work for several clients from Germany on a day-by-day basis. I have completed various projects in the industrial and commercial sectors and accumulated extensive experience with German construction methods, practices, and standards.

With more than 10 years’ experience in steel structures, involved in various aspects of the construction business, a good work-ethic, self-discipline, and determination, I am able to deliver top-quality results in a timely manner across all stages of a project to my clients.

I have the required computer equipment, software, and other infrastructure for independent execution.
